Google Developer Students Club, NIE

We're _

We, Developer Student Clubs are initiative taken by Google Developers taken to bridge the gap between theory and application. DSC started in India in 2017 and now our community spreads across the whole Globe. At a base level, we are university-based community groups for students interested in Google developer technologies. Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome. By joining a DSC, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community.
